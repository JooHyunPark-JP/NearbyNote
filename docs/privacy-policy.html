Privacy Policy — NearbyNote

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

NearbyNote (“the App”) provides location-based reminders on Android. We design the App to minimize data leaving your device. We do not operate any server backend.

1) Data We Access/Process

Location (precise/approximate, including background)

Used to create and trigger your geofences and to display your position on the map.

Network & map requests

Required to load map tiles and perform place search via Mapbox. Requests can include your IP address and app/device metadata needed to provide these services.

Microphone (optional, when you start voice input)

The App invokes the device’s built-in speech recognition service (e.g., Google Speech Services). Audio is handled by that provider according to your device settings. The App does not capture or store audio.

Local app data (notes, saved places, geofence settings)

Stored only on your device using local storage.

The App does not include ads or third-party analytics SDKs.

2) How We Use the Data

  • Provide core features: geofence notifications, maps, place search, and optional voice input.
  • Ensure reliability in the background and after reboot using Android Location & Geofencing APIs (Google Play services), a foreground service, and a boot receiver.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

3) Third-Party Services

To deliver specific features, the App interacts with:

  • Mapbox — map tiles and geocoding/place search. The Mapbox SDK and related services may process diagnostic or usage information in accordance with Mapbox’s privacy notice.
  • Device speech recognition service (e.g., Google Speech Services) — used only when you start voice input; processing may occur on-device or by the provider per device capabilities and settings.
  • Android/Google Play services (Location & Geofencing APIs) — system components on your device that determine location and deliver geofence enter/exit events to the App.

We do not receive your notes or transcripts on any server we operate.

4) Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties for their independent marketing. Limited transfers occur only as necessary to deliver maps/geocoding and, if invoked, speech recognition. Platform services (Android/Google Play services) may process location to provide system features.

5) Data Retention & Deletion

  • On-device data (notes, geofences, saved places) remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Third-party processing (Mapbox, device speech service, platform location services) follows each provider’s retention practices.
  • You can delete items in the App or uninstall to remove local data.

6) Your Choices & Controls

  • Permissions — You can grant/deny location, microphone, and notifications at runtime and later change them in Android Settings.
  • Background location — Requested only to deliver geofence reminders; if you remove all location-based notes, background location is no longer needed.
  • Maps & network — Maps and place search require network connectivity; disabling permissions or network may limit features.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App targets a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8) Security

All network communication uses HTTPS. Android cloud backup for the App is disabled to reduce unintended copies of local data. We do not operate a remote server for your notes.

9) International Processing

Our providers (e.g., Mapbox, device/OS services) may process data on servers outside your country. Refer to each provider’s privacy notice for details.

10) Changes

We may update this Policy; the “Last Updated” date will reflect the latest version.
